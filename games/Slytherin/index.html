Slytherin

A two player thriller
Instructions:
Player Red: Press 'W' 'A' 'S'&npsp;'D'  to move the snake.
Player Blue: Press arrow keys(←↑→↓) to move the snake
Rules:
When a snake 2x larger than the other attacks, the other snake dies.
When a snake less than 2x larger than the other attacks, the other reduces length
Get two times bigger and kill the other snake by attacking it!
